Just like the spider, I'm a web developer too

I've been working as a web devloper for the past 14 years or so, and it is something I love doing. This site is mainly for testing out new web stuff that's coming along like Html5, Css3, and new features of JavaScript.

But there's also other stuff about me here, so if you feel interested please do browse around.
