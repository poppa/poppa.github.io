Method mkmapping()

Method mkmapping

mapping mkmapping(array ind, array val)

Description

Make a mapping from two arrays.

Makes a mapping ind[x]:val[x], 0 <= x < sizeof(ind).

ind and val must have the same size.

This is the inverse operation of indices() and values().
